# Eliud Kipchoge's Record Marathon Pace

## Source
Based on Wikipedia article for Eliud Kipchoge (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eliud_Kipchoge)

## Key Facts
- Eliud Kipchoge set the official world record in the marathon at the 2018 Berlin Marathon
- His record time: 2 hours, 1 minute, 39 seconds (2:01:39)
- Marathon distance: 42.195 km (standard marathon distance)

## Calculation of Speed
- Total time in seconds: 2 × 3600 + 1 × 60 + 39 = 7200 + 60 + 39 = 7299 seconds
- Total time in hours: 7299 / 3600 = 2.0275 hours
- Speed = Distance / Time = 42.195 km / 2.0275 hours = 20.81 km/h (approximately)

## Pace
- Pace per km: 2:01:39 / 42.195 = approximately 2 minutes 53 seconds per km
