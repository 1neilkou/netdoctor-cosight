# Search Results Summary - Step 0: Girls Who Code Statistics

## Source: Girls Who Code About Us Page (girlswhocode.com/about-us)

### Key Data Found:
- **1995**: 37% of computer scientists were women
- **2017**: 24% of computer scientists were women  
- **2022**: 22% of computer scientists were women

### Direct Quote from Page:
"In 1995, 37% of computer scientists were women. Today, it's only 24%. The percent will continue to decline if we do nothing."

### Data Visualization on Page:
The page shows a timeline with three data points:
- 1995: 37%
- 2017: 24%
- 2022: 22%

### Analysis:
Starting from 37% in 1995, a change of 13 percentage points brings us to 24% (37 - 13 = 24).
The data shows 24% was reached in 2017.
Time elapsed: 2017 - 1995 = 22 years.
