# Final Answer Compilation

## Question
As of the end of the 44th season of the American version of Survivor, how many more unique winners have there been compared to the number of winners of American Idol?

## Analysis

### Survivor (US) - Unique Winners
- Total seasons aired (through season 44): 44
- Repeat winners: 2
  - Sandra Diaz-Twine: Seasons 7 (Pearl Islands) and 20 (Heroes vs. Villains)
  - Tony Vlachos: Seasons 28 (Cagayan) and 40 (Winners at War)
- Unique winners: 44 - 2 = **42**

### American Idol - Winners
- Total seasons aired (through season 23): 23
- Repeat winners: 0 (each season has a different winner)
- Total unique winners: **23**

### Difference
- Survivor unique winners (42) - American Idol winners (23) = **19**

## Final Answer
19 more unique winners.

FINAL ANSWER: 19
