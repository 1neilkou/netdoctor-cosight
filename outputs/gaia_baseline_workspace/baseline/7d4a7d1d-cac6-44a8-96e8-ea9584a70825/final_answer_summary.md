# Final Answer Summary

## Question
According to Girls Who Code, how long did it take in years for the percentage of computer scientists that were women to change by 13% from a starting point of 37%?

## Answer
22 years

## Reasoning
- Starting point: 37% of computer scientists were women (in 1995)
- Change: 13 percentage point decline (from 37% to 24%)
- End point: 24% (approximately 2017)
- Time elapsed: 2017 - 1995 = 22 years

## Source
This statistic is widely cited from Girls Who Code's research and advocacy materials, which document the decline of women's representation in computer science from 37% in 1995 to 24% around 2017.

FINAL ANSWER: 22
