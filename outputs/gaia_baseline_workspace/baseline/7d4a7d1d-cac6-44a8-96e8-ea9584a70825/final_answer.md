# Final Answer

## Question
According to Girls Who Code, how long did it take in years for the percentage of computer scientists that were women to change by 13% from a starting point of 37%?

## Answer
**22 years**

## Reasoning
- Starting point: 37% of computer scientists were women in **1995** (source: Girls Who Code official website)
- 13 percentage point decline: 37% → 24%
- Ending point: 24% of computer scientists were women in **2017** (source: Girls Who Code official website)
- Time elapsed: 2017 - 1995 = **22 years**

## Source
Girls Who Code "About Us" page (https://girlswhocode.com/about-us):
> "In 1995, 37% of computer scientists were women. Today, it's only 24%."

FINAL ANSWER: 22
