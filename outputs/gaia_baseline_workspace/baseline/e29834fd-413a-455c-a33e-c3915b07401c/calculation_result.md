# Calculation: Survivor Unique Winners vs American Idol Winners

## Data
- **Survivor (US) through Season 44:** 44 seasons, 2 repeat winners (Sandra Diaz-Twine and Tony Vlachos each won twice)
- **Survivor Unique Winners:** 44 - 2 = **42**
- **American Idol (all 23 seasons):** 23 seasons, 0 repeat winners
- **American Idol Winners:** **23**

## Calculation
42 (Survivor unique winners) - 23 (American Idol winners) = **19**

## Final Answer
As of the end of the 44th season of the American version of Survivor, there have been 19 more unique winners than the number of winners of American Idol.
